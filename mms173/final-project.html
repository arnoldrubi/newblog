Hey! I'm Arnold.

I am currently taking up BA Multimedia Studies at the University of the Philippines Open University. Aside from being an “Iskolar ng Bayan”, I am a web designer and manages my own company. This short or rather small space online is dedicated to the photographs I took during my stint with MMS 173 (Photography in Multimedia). I’ve learned a lot in this course, and it helped me appreciate more the art of photography.

Welcome to my Photo Gallery

A friend loaned to me his Fujifilm XT-20 for this project. I started learning the controls first before heading out and shoot. I had fun using mirrorless camera because it’s lightweight and the learning curve is low. Although I must admit that I sometimes miss the bulky feel of DSLRs.

I decided not to spend a lot of time on post processing. I only used cropping, levels, and contrasts on some images in this gallery. I tried to train myself to polish my framing and knowledge in the exposure trinity (shutter, aperture, and ISO) and not to rely much on post processing.

The Sights and Smiles of Plaridel

Plaridel is a town in Bulacan, a province north of Metro Manila. This is where my wife EJ and I started to build our family. It is a laid-back town, contrasting the busy and city-like vibe of my birthplace – Baliuag. I was only able to capture scenes that are near our home. This was because of the constraint in time and mobility. I hope I’ll be able to get to out more and take photos of other places in our town.
